about

Sometimes I smile at myself (in the mirror) and wonder what must it be like to be my friend.

Co-founder of Joyus Studio, a storytelling and experience design consultancy, with Divya Tak. Currently also building Klydo, a fashion platform in India (2025), and General Partner at Tres Monos Capital, an early-stage fund focused on underrepresented entrepreneurs (founded 2017).

I studied computer science at Tufts and culinary arts at the French Culinary Institute in New York; I am currently pursuing an MFA in Writing and Poetics at the Jack Kerouac School of Disembodied Poetics, Naropa University. My career started in technical program management before moving to product and marketing — consulting with companies like Microsoft, McGraw-Hill, and TUI Travel across the US, UK, and India. From 2018–2019, I was CEO of Indian edTech company Vidyanext.

In 2022, I published my first poetry collection, How the World Works. That same year, I married Gourav in Jaisalmer. We live between Bangalore and New York.
