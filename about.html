about

I’m a storyteller and creative consultant, excited about finding and growing the beauty in the world. I love language and emotional experiences, and I specialize in bringing complex ideas to life.

Currently building Klydo, a fashion platform in India. General Partner at Tres Monos Capital, an early-stage fund focused on underrepresented entrepreneurs. Co-founder of Joyus Studio, a storytelling and experience design consultancy.

I studied computer science at Tufts and culinary arts at the French Culinary Institute in New York. My career started in technical program management before moving to product and marketing — consulting with companies like Microsoft, McGraw-Hill, and TUI Travel across the US, UK, and India. From 2018–2019, I was CEO of Indian edTech company Vidyanext.

In 2022, I published my first poetry collection, How the World Works. That same year, I married Gourav in Jaisalmer. We live between Bangalore and New York.
