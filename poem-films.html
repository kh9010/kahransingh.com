poem films

What happens to a poem when you give it a body? Each of these poems was set to two entirely different visual worlds — same words, different feeling.

When I Dance with You

a sunset room · a city at night

Every Time You Wink at Me

an abstract swirl · a luminous figure

In My Heart Something Called to the Rain

rain on still water · a rainy street

I Saw Something Beautiful Today

a golden field · a figure in the dark

Made with Divya Tak. Kahran wrote the poems and generated image prompts with MidJourney; Divya built the video generation pipeline using Stable Diffusion and ComfyUI. An experiment in how different visuals change the feeling of the same words. Selected for SEEN V, a curated exhibition by Creativity Alive Boulder, exhibited at Boulder Public Library.
