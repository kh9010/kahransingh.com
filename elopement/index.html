Telluride, December 2021

“We threw our original timeline out the window and just went with the flow. It didn’t feel like getting ready photos, it felt like 3 friends hanging out and simply having the best time.” — Henry Tieu

In December 2021, Kahran and Gourav eloped in Telluride, Colorado — just the two of them, in the snow, in Indian dress. A year later, they celebrated with family and friends in Jaisalmer.

Two ceremonies. One in the mountains, quiet and cold and entirely theirs. One in the desert, loud and warm and entirely everyone’s.
