Our Story

"Our story began in Sadashivnagar, under orange lights and in empty roads. A time when the land is very dry, summer is yet to come, and the rains are yet to start. By April, we were found, in that brief wisp of spring, that Bangalore gets, sometimes, somewhere, somehow."

"After that, what is love, but a set of choices made with a little more sparkle"

"a little more joy, maybe"

"a little more wonder, maybe"

"Thinking just a few days ahead of where you used to think before."

"After that first year we thought, 'This is it!' Time to scurry home And find our stories again Don't let fling become fantasy..."

"But somehow this reality never got mashed these days are still like that first day when we wondered was this fantasy or is this a real life..."

"Telling any story takes choosing an arbitrary beginning and end In a grand story that has no beginning and end..."

## Events (/events)

**Page Title:** Events — G&K

### Text Content

Events

Below you'll find details about our three day celebration in Jaisalmer, India! Since this is a destination wedding we're still putting details together, please check back in as we add more updates!

#### Thursday - 15th December 2022

**Thursday AM:** All guests arrive in Jaisalmer! We can't wait to welcome you with high tea, snacks and a special cocktail or two — to get you in the wedding spirit!

**Thursday PM:** We will kick off the weekend of celebrations with a very special sunset dinner deep in the sand dunes of Jaisalmer. Shuttles will begin departures at 16:00. Dress code - Casual and Semi casual Indian / Western in shades of Maroon or Black. Suggested footwear: Boots or Barefoot (you will be on sand)

#### Friday - 16th December 2022

**Friday AM:** Kick off your morning with our Haldi in the Courtyard, and then adorn your hands with Henna at our Mehendi in the late morning and early afternoon. You will then have a few hours to rest! Dress code - Indian semi formals in Ivory or shades of Mustard. Suggested footwear: Open toe sandals/ relaxed footwear

**Friday PM:** An evening of dance and music at our Sangeet in the Lakeside Garden! Dress code - Glamorous Indian / Western formals in jewel tones. Note: black (onyx), is a jewel tone. Suggested footwear: Dressy & glam, avoid Stilettos

#### Saturday - 17th December 2022

**Saturday AM:** A morning to explore the Fort and Jaisalmer city.

**Saturday PM:** We will approach the wedding hour with a very special set of performances, followed by the wedding itself. A reception will follow, to allow for appropriate levels of dinner, dancing and general merriment! Dress Code - Indian / Western formals in Pastel shades or Tan. Suggested footwear: Formal

#### Sunday - 18th December 2022

**Sunday AM:** A Sunday brunch and a final chance to run around Jaisalmer (and perhaps a bit of sand surfing, for the un-hungover and daring few?) before we checkout and head on!

**12:00 noon:** Guests checkout
