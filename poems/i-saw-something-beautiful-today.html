I saw something beautiful today
scroll
I saw something beautiful today It gives me shivers to think of it For to let yourself see beauty Is a special gift Not to be taken By the giver Or the gived.
And hark! Yes For this was beauty Clad and a shimmer of red Brown against the sun Resplendent agent of life Oh Creator I praise you I salute you What creations you have made.
I watch this boy come to end life Or bring it So innocent is wonder So innocent is life
Oh, you shimmer of Red. Shimmer me brighter, you.
Shimmer me wonder, you.
Whether I die in peace is my choice. Whether I die at all, maybe yours God bless you Child of creation Child of wonder Child of tears
