Anuraag oh Anuraag
scroll
Anuraag oh Anuraag Tell me where will you go? You are caught and snared Thrashing about Will only cause your predicament to grow
Anuraag oh Anuraag Oh now, what shall be done? The chase is caught, Secured you are mine, As much as the one secures The river which continues to run
Anuraag, oh Anuraag. Snared, you are to me. And so we shall sit, And wait in discontent To see what will be Will be
