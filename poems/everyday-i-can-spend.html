Everyday I Can Spend
scroll
Everyday I can spend in the company of those whom I love I count a day thankful.
There will come a time, I know there will come a time, that our time will be lost in this and that, in what could be and what was, there will be times when days are lost to what must be and what was. That will be.
But today, I get to spend time with my lovers. My friends. My joys. My life, manifest in people.
Our health may not be for this world. Our lives may not be for this world. But help me if there is not tomorrow. Help me if there is no memory before today. Help me if what is today’s tomorrow is actually just today.
Today is a day in the company of those I love. And lord love bless So tomorrow be.
