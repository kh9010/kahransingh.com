A Wedding Toast
scroll
All I know, my friends Is that terrible heartbreak I would feel When I left a place that held me close and made me feel that this is this is that there is there is I can be that when I left a place Like that
There would be a curious heartbreak A terrible heartbreak A feeling of doubt and wonder and fear and loneliness and yes wonder again but so much doubt
But somehow what I feel now Is just that wonder With maybe a little more space for awe Mixed in Where that godforsaken heavy doubt used to be.
To you, my friends, all the love in the world.
