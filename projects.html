BLOSSOM

Interactive Installation — with Divya Tak — In development

A touchscreen installation where visitors assemble a poem by choosing between emotional poles — grip or release, smother or howl — drawing from 230 fragments written across several years of poetry. Each choice shapes a generative flower; every flower joins a living garden of all previous visitors' poems. Three kinds of intelligence are tangled in every line: the poet's, the machine's, the visitor's. Submitted to Science Gallery Bengaluru's INTELLIGENCE open call.

Poem Films

Video — with Divya Tak — 2024

An experiment in how different visuals change the feeling of a poem. For each poem, two entirely different video interpretations — one with rain drops, one with a rain-soaked street; one in an open field, one with a figure in shadows. Made using MidJourney and Stable Diffusion.
