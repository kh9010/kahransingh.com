Kahran & Gourav

A celebration of love — Jaisalmer, December 2022

Four days in the Golden City — from sunset dinners in the sand dunes, to haldi in the courtyard, sangeet by the lake, and a wedding under the Rajasthani sky. 150 guests flew in from around the world to celebrate at Suryagarh, Jaisalmer.
